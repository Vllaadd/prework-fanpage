Bio

X Ambassadors (also stylized XA) are an American rock band from Ithaca, New York.
Its members currently include lead vocalist Sam Harris, keyboardist Casey Harris, and drummer Adam Levin.
Their most notable songs include "Jungle", "Renegades", and "Unsteady". The band's debut full-length album,
VHS, was released on June 30, 2015.
In an interview with Caitlin White, X Ambassadors declared themselves distinctly rock with few characteristics
commonly associated with indie bands.[3] They referenced Incubus and the Red Hot Chili Peppers as heroes.

The bend has released four albums:

  • Ambassadors and Litost
  • Love Songs Drug Songs and The Reason
  • VHS
  • Orion
